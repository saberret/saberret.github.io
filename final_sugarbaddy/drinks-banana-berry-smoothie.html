Banana Berry SmoothieYield: 2 1/2 servings. Serving size: 1 cup.

Preparation Time: 5 min

Ingredients

1 cup ice cubes
1 medium banana (approximately 6 inches long),
sliced and frozen
1/2 cup frozen blueberries (not thawed)
1 container (8 ounces) nonfat, artificially sweetened vanilla yogurt
1/4 cup skim milk

Directions

Place ice cubes at the bottom of the blender and add banana slices, blueberries, yogurt, and milk.
Cover and blend in pulses until smooth, stopping frequently to stir down the ice.
Serve right away.
Freeze leftovers in an airtight, microwave-safe container and thaw in the microwave until slushy.

Nutrition Facts Per ServingEquivalent to eating 1 1/2 fruit and drinking 1/2 cup skim milk

Calories: 138
Carbohydrates: 28 g
Protein: 6 g
Fat: < 1 g
Sodium: 80 mg
Fiber: 3 g
