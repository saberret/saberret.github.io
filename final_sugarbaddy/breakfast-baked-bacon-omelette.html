Baked Bacon Omelette

Preparation Time: 5 min
Cooking Time: 20 min
Yield: 1 serving
Serving size: 1

Ingredients

2 eggs
150 g bacon cut in cubes
75 g butter
50 g fresh spinach
1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh chives (optional)
salt and pepper

Directions

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
Grease an individual serving-sized baking dish with butter.
Fry bacon and spinach in the remaining butter.
Whisk the eggs until frothy.
Mix in the spinach and bacon, including the fat left from frying.
Add some finely chopped chives.
Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Pour the egg mixture into baking dish(es) and bake for 20 minutes or until set and golden brown.
Let cool for a few minutes and serve.

Nutrition Facts Per Serving

Carbohydrates: 3 g
