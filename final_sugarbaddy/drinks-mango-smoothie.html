Mango SmoothieYield: 4 servings. Serving size: 5 oz.

Preparation Time: 5 min

Ingredients

2 cups frozen mango chunks, plus additional for garnish
2 containers (6 ounces each) low-fat vanilla yogurt
3/4 cup orange juice
1 teaspoon vanilla (optional)
Juice of 1/2 lime
Pinch salt

Directions

Combine 2 cups mango chunks, yogurt, orange juice, vanilla, if desired, lime juice, and salt in blender; blend until smooth.
Pour into four glasses.
Garnish with additional mango chunks.

Nutrition Facts Per ServingEquivalent to 1 1/2 fruit

Calories: 93
Carbohydrates: 21 g
Protein: 3 g
Fat: 1 g
Sodium: 39 mg
Fiber: 1 g
