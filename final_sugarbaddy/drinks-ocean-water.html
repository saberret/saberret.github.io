Ocean WaterYield: 1 serving. Serving size: 6 oz.

Preparation Time: 2 min

Ingredients

1/2 cup (4 oz) white grape juice, chilled
2 drops blue food coloring
1/4 cup (2 oz) club soda, chilled

Directions

Pour white grape juice into a drinking glass.
Add food coloring, and mix well with a spoon.
Pour in club soda and stir 3-4 times to mix.
Drink right away.
