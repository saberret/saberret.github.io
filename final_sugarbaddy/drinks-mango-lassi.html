Mango LassiYield: 1 liter or 4 servings. Serving size: 1 cup.

Preparation Time: 5 min

Ingredients

1 cup mango nectar
1/2 cup skim milk
1/2 cup plain nonfat yogurt
1 tsp Cyrstal Light Mango Passionate Liquid Drink Mix
1 1/2 cups ice cubes

Directions

Blend all ingredients except ice cubes in blender until drink mix is dissolved.
Add ice; blend on high speed until smooth.
Serve immediately over ice.
