Hot CocoaYield: 3 3/4 cups. Serving size: 3/4 cup.

Preparation Time: 5 min

Ingredients

1/4 cup cocoa
5 tablespoons sugar
Dash salt
1/2 cup hot water
3 cups skim milk
1/4 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

Combine cocoa, sugar, and salt in a medium saucepan.
Gradually add hot water and boil over medium heat for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
Add skim milk and heat thoroughly, stirring frequently.
Do not allow the mixture to boil.
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Serve hot.
