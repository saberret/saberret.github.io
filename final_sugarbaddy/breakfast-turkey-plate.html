Turkey Plate

Preparation Time: 5 min
Yield: 2 servings
Serving size: 1

Ingredients

175 g deli turkey
2 avocados
75 g cream cheese
50 g lettuce
60 ml olive oil
salt and pepper

Directions

Put the turkey, sliced avocado, lettuce and cream cheese on a plate.
Drizzle olive oil over the vegetables and season to taste with salt and pepper.

Nutrition Facts Per Serving

Carbohydrates: 9 g
